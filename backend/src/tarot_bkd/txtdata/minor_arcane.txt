### Cups

1. **Ace of Cups**:
   - meanings upright: New feelings, spirituality, intuition, love
   - meanings reversed: Blocked or repressed emotions, lost opportunity

2. **Two of Cups**:
   - meanings upright: Unified love, partnership, mutual attraction
   - meanings reversed: Break-up, imbalance in a relationship, tension

3. **Three of Cups**:
   - meanings upright: Celebration, friendship, creativity, community
   - meanings reversed: Overindulgence, gossip, isolation

4. **Four of Cups**:
   - meanings upright: Apathy, contemplation, disconnectedness, dissatisfaction
   - meanings reversed: Suddenly seeing a new opportunity, feeling motivated

5. **Five of Cups**:
   - meanings upright: Loss, regret, disappointment, focusing on the negative
   - meanings reversed: Acceptance, moving on, finding peace

6. **Six of Cups**:
   - meanings upright: Nostalgia, childhood memories, innocence, joy
   - meanings reversed: Stuck in the past, naivety, unrealistic expectations

7. **Seven of Cups**:
   - meanings upright: Choices, confusion, daydreaming, illusion
   - meanings reversed: Clarity, decision-making, seeing through deceit

8. **Eight of Cups**:
   - meanings upright: Abandonment, moving on, search for meaning
   - meanings reversed: Fear of moving on, stagnation, clinging to the past

9. **Nine of Cups**:
   - meanings upright: Satisfaction, emotional stability, luxury
   - meanings reversed: Dissatisfaction, materialism, feeling unfulfilled

10. **Ten of Cups**:
    - meanings upright: Emotional fulfillment, happiness, harmony
    - meanings reversed: Broken family, disharmony, unhappiness

11. **Page of Cups**:
    - meanings upright: Creativity, receiving a message, intuitive
    - meanings reversed: Emotional immaturity, insecurity, disappointment

12. **Knight of Cups**:
    - meanings upright: Romance, charm, 'knight in shining armor'
    - meanings reversed: Disillusionment, the allure fading, moodiness

13. **Queen of Cups**:
    - meanings upright: Compassion, caring, emotional stability
    - meanings reversed: Dependency, smothering, manipulation

14. **King of Cups**:
    - meanings upright: Emotional balance, generosity, diplomacy
    - meanings reversed: Manipulation, moodiness, volatility
